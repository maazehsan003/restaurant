Our Story

A legacy of culinary excellence and timeless elegance

The Velvet Table Legacy

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2009 by renowned chef Alexandre Rousseau, The Velvet Table was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a dining sanctuary where culinary artistry and refined hospitality converge.

What began as an intimate 20-seat establishment has blossomed into a globally acclaimed destination, earning five Michelin stars and the hearts of food enthusiasts worldwide. Yet despite our growth, we've never lost sight of what matters most—creating extraordinary moments through exceptional food.

Every dish we serve carries the weight of our commitment to excellence, the creativity of our talented team, and the stories of the artisans and farmers who supply our ingredients.
